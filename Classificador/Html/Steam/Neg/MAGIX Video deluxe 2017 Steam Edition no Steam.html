Sobre este software

No matter your skill level or whether you're a beginner or an advanced user, Video deluxe 2017 - Steam Edition meets the full spectrum of video editing needs. Easily create impressive videos using intuitive tools such as the world's best image stabilization tool Mercalli V2 from proDAD and take advantage of unique features such as HEVC support and storyboard and timeline modes. And that's not all - over 1,500 great effects are included.

The most important features:

[/list]
  • More than 1,500 effects, transitions, intros/outros and music
  • Creative freedom: Up to 32 multimedia tracks
  • Automatic video editing to the beat of the music
  • Perfect image stabilization thanks to proDAD Mercalli V2
  • Choose from either timeline or storyboard mode
  • SD, HD, UHD, 4K: Edit all resolutions up to 4K
  • Share directly online on YouTube, Facebook and Vimeo

Video editing for every skill level:


For the essentials: Storyboard mode
Select your best scenes, add transitions and set to music. This mode saves a ton of time, so it's great for more advanced users too.
For more details: Timeline mode
Develop your full creative potential on 32 multimedia tracks with Video deluxe 2017 – Steam Edition.Intros/outros, transitions, titles, effects – all in perfect harmony with your recordings.


Discover the fun way to edit videos


Automatic optimization
Save time: Video deluxe 2017 – Steam Edition optimizes color, white balance and saturation in your videos fully automatically.
Automatic video editing
Save even more time: Let the program analyze your recordings and cut them automatically if needed.
Disc menus
Create DVDs and Blu-ray Discs using Video deluxe 2017 – Steam Edition. The program also includes tons of animated menu templates.
Vimeo – the online community
Share your videos directly from the program with the Vimeo worldwide community. This premium video platform is ad-free and lets your video shine in HD quality. What are you waiting for?

Requisitos de sistema

    Mínimos:
    • SO: Windows 7 (64-bit)
    • Processador: 2.4 GHz
    • Memória: 6 GB de RAM
    • Placa de vídeo: Onboard, min. resolution 1024x768
    • Armazenamento: 2 GB de espaço disponível
    • Placa de som: Internal sound card
    • Outras observações: Supported import formats Video: (DV-)AVI, HEVC/H.265*, M(2)TS, MJPEG, MKV, MOV, MPEG-1/2/4, MXV, VOB, WMV(HD) Audio: MIDI, MP3, OGG, Surround sound/5.1, WAV, WMA Image: BMP, GIF, JPEG, TGA, TIF Supported export formats Disc: DVD, Blu-ray Disc, AVCHD disc Video: (DV-)AVI, HEVC/H.265*, MJPEG, MOV, MPEG-1/2/4, MXV, WMV(HD) Audio: MP3, Surround sound/5.1, WAV Image: BMP, JPEG *HEVC encoding requires fee-based activation. MP3 export requires installation of Windows Media Player Version 10 or higher.
    Recomendados:
    • SO: Windows 7 | 8 | 10 (64-bit)
    • Processador: 2.8 GHz
    • Memória: 8 GB de RAM
    • Placa de vídeo: Dedicated, at least 512 MB VRAM
    • Armazenamento: 2 GB de espaço disponível
    • Placa de som: Multi-channel sound card recommended for surround sound editing
    • Outras observações: Some advanced program functions demand more of your computer's processing power. To get the most from these functions, your system should at least meet the recommended requirements. Check your operating system’s control panel for technical information about your computer.
